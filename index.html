About Angela

Certified in pre and postnatal coaching, and continuing my education in all things woman, and movement therapy coaching, I help women feel fit, strong and empowered through all ages and stages of life through both fitness and self-defence based training. I am passionate about delivering a quality service to the women of Christchurch, particularly those struggling with their fitness during pregnancy, post –pregnancy and menopause. Helping women (and men!) with pelvic floor dysfunction find their strength again is a personal crusade of mine, as I strongly believe that having issues like these does not make you BROKEN! There is absolutely life to live and exercise to do when you are carrying a pelvic floor injury or abdominal separation. I am an accredited trainer with Protect Self Defence, NZ’s number one provider of personal protection education and my favourite part of this work is working with women and young people to feel safe, strong and empowered to live their best life.

On a personal level, I am Mum to two gorgeous young children and wife to a super nice guy (who also coaches in the self-defence side of this work). I have a background in ITF Taekwon-Do (a fancy kicking martial art), having just owned my own TKD school up to June of 2019, when I sold the club to focus more on my family and development of Pulse – Training for Life. I was training for my 4th dan grading while I was pregnant with my youngest, in hindsight, I realised that I hadn’t really been training in a safe and effective way during my pregnancy and the postpartum period. I was diagnosed in 2018 with a 2-3 degree pelvic organ prolapse which I have been working hard to recover from and rebuild my strength to get back to the training that I love so dearly. Because of this, I’ve made it my mission to get SUPER well educated about this and pass that knowledge on to you, so you can have a better experience and faster recovery than I did!
