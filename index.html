Activities

Pulse Training For Life, based in Christchurch, offers a range of activities, including ITF Taekwon-Do, personal or small group fitness training, and self defence workshops

TKD Kubz

Our TKD Kubz programme is a specially designed class for children aged between 3-6 years of age.

TKD Kids

Junior Taekwon-Do programme for children aged between 5-9 years of age.

TKD Youth / Adults

No matter what your current fitness level, our instructors are trained to give you safe and enjoyable tuition.

Personal Training

Specialised coaching focused on pre-postnatal women, and men & women with pelvic floor issues. From a qualified pre/post natal coach.

Self Defence

Our coaches are accredited by Protect Self Defence, the leading provider of self defence workshops in New Zealand.
